Product Brief

Product Brief

Before you start recording, take 3 minutes to understand what this feature does, where it works, and what you must communicate accurately to your audience.

🌐
What It Does

Immersive Translate now officially supports the Steam desktop client. Through a simple developer-mode installation, players get bilingual side-by-side translation across Steam's store pages, Workshop, and community boards — plus instant input translation.

At its core, it's a browser extension loaded into Steam's built-in Chromium engine via developer mode — bringing full translation capability directly into the Steam client.

✨
Key Selling Points
  • ✓ Unique bilingual display — original and translated text side by side, no reading disruption
  • ✓ Free to use, powered by 20+ top AI engines: ChatGPT, DeepL, DeepSeek, Gemini and more
  • ✓ Lightweight and invisible — no impact on Steam client performance or load speed
  • ✓ Triple-space shortcut: type in any language, press space 3 times, auto-translates to your target language
  • ✓ Privacy-first: translated content is never stored or used for AI training
🎯
What Can You Actually Do With It?
🏪
Read store pages fluently — Game descriptions, patch notes, and system requirements all appear in bilingual view. No more guessing before you buy.
🔧
Conquer Workshop mods — Mod installation guides and compatibility notes translated instantly. Even the most complex Workshop setups become manageable.
💬
Read global player reviews — Browse international reviews, guides, and discussions without missing a single insight.
⌨️
Post in any language — Type in English, hit space 3 times, and your message is instantly translated. Engage with global players effortlessly.
📋
What to Mention in Your Video
  • ✓ The install requires "Developer Mode" — this is a normal step, not a hack
  • ✓ It translates the Steam client interface (store, community, Workshop) — not the game itself
  • ✓ Supports 100+ languages — translate any language into English, or between any two languages
  • ✓ Core features are completely free; Pro unlocks more precise AI translation strategies
  • ✓ Over 20 million users worldwide trust this tool
⚠️
Current Limitations (Please Tell Your Audience)
  • ! In-game text is not translated: This feature only covers the Steam client interface — not in-game dialogue, UI, or subtitles
  • ! Store videos not supported: Due to Steam's embedded video player, AI subtitles for store trailers are not yet available

Being upfront about limitations actually builds trust. Your audience will respect your honesty — and won't feel misled later.

Quick Start

Creator Quick-Start Guide

From install to recording footage — just 4 steps, under 5 minutes. This is a streamlined version built specifically for creators, with the redundant steps removed.

🎮
01
📦
Download the Package
Visit the official page and download the latest plugin package. It's a .zip file — unzip it to find the .crx file inside.
Download Free →
02
🪟
Open Steam in a Separate Window
Open and log in to Steam. Right-click any game and select "Open in new tab" (or hold Ctrl and click) to open a standalone Steam window.
03
🔧
Open Extensions Manager
In the new window's address bar, paste the URL below and press Enter. Then toggle on Developer Mode in the top-right corner.
chrome://extensions/
04
🎉
Drag & Drop to Install
Drag the unzipped .crx file directly into the extensions page. After installing, close Steam and reopen it. Refresh a few times — when the floating ball appears on the right, you're all set!
01
🌐
Visit the Extension Store
In Chrome / Edge / Firefox / Safari, search for "Immersive Translate" and click Install.
Chrome Web Store →
02
⚙️
Enable the Extension
After installing, click the extension icon in the top-right corner of your browser, enable Immersive Translate, and log in.
03
🎉
Open Steam in Your Browser
Visit store.steampowered.com in your browser to experience full translation. Note: the browser version doesn't work inside the Steam desktop client.
💡
Recording tip: After installing, capture a "before vs. after" comparison on the store page, Workshop, and review section — these are your most compelling footage. The triple-space input trick is also perfect for a short-form "wow moment."
